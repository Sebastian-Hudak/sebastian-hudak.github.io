Building Rockets Like Cars

Rocket Factory Augsburg AG is one of the first commercial European companies developing orbital class launcher capable of delivering variety of payloads to different orbits. Its uniqueness stands in successful development of Europe's first oxygen-rich staged combustion (ORSC) engine called HELIX capable of delivering roughly 100kN of thrust.

Watch the 2023 IST campaign video for a quick look at the companies’ growth.

Everyday Work

As a part of propulsion R&D team I have made contributions in fields of fluid mechanics analysis on refinement and development of HELIX specificaly cavitation research.

Key projects I have been involved in include:

Iterations of system design, cavitation research of propellant/ignition feed system, development of test rigs for component testing, and empirical data analysis.

RFA's unconventional approach to aerospace component design well aligned with my passion for innovation and problem-solving, allowing me to apply and expand my skills further.

Unique oportunity to stand behind design of HELIX engine components on the whole design jurney.
